About

I am a Theoretical Physicist, currently pursuing a PhD at the College of Natural Sciences and Mathematics in University of Houston. By the way, check out my awesome work.

I am interested in the quantum nature of gravity, the geometrical anatomy and the emergence of spacetime. Dualities in physics is another gamechanging idea that fascinates me a lot. I use tools and principles of Quantum Field Thery, String Theory and mathematical physics. More recently I have developed interest in the connection between Quantum Information Science and the origin of spacetime.
